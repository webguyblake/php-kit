\Prismic\Fragment\Span

\Prismic\Fragment\SpanHyperlinkSpan

This class embodies a link span.

A span comes in a array of spans, which is served with a raw text. If the raw text is "Hello world!", and the HyperlinkSpan's start is 6 and its end is 11, then the piece that is meant to be the hypertext is "world".
