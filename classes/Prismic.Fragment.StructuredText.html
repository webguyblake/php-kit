\Prismic\Fragment

\Prismic\FragmentStructuredText

This class embodies a StructuredText fragment.

Technically, a StructuredText fragment is not much more than an array of blocks, but there are many things to do with this fragment, including in the HTML serialization, but not only. It is arguably the most powerful and manipulable way any CMS stores structured text nowadays.
